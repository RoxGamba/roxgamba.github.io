This past week I was at AEI Potsdam, to visit Alessandra Buonanno's group and give a seminar about horizon absorption (yes,
the same one I gave at UVA a few weeks back!).

This was my first time in Potsdam, and I had a [fabulous](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-FLnQrUGTc&list=RDx-FLnQrUGTc&start_radio=1) time: Potsdam is very cute, and AEI itself pretty impressive. I got to chat with a number of very cool people, both senior (Tim, Jan, Alessandra herself) and less so (Felix, Marcus, Gonzalo, Arnab, Ollie, Chen, ...).
Special mention to my bestie [Koustav](https://www.aei.mpg.de/person/149857/2784) (and Marcus as well!) who not only showed me around, but also agreed to join me __very__ last minute in attending a [Madison Beer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XFR7v5ix5hU&list=RDXFR7v5ix5hU&start_radio=1) concert.

Until next time, Germany!

P.S: I had so much Club Mate, folks. So much.
